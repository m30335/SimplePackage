Threat of New Entrants

The ease or difficulty of new companies entering the market

High threat can lower profitability

Bargaining Power of Suppliers

The degree to which suppliers can influence the price and quality of goods or services

High bargaining power can increase costs

Bargaining Power of Buyers

The degree to which buyers can influence the price and quality of goods or services

High bargaining power can decrease prices

Threat of Substitutes

The extent to which alternative products or services can be used in place of a company's products or services

High threat can reduce demand

Rivalry Among Existing Competitors

The degree of competition among companies in the same industry

High rivalry can lower profitability
